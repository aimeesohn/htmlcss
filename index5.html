HN @Shanghai meetup

What

This meetup is dedicated to people who could be readers of Hacker News, really anybody who would be interested by the kind of news this community share; technologists, geeks, entrepreneurs... Just come for discussing your own project, the latest trends or simply meeting with likely minded folks.

主题

我们的聚会没有特定的主题。无论您是 Hacker News 或类似新闻的读者，或是某项技术的专家、爱好者，抑或是企业家等等，都非常欢迎您来与我们讨论各种您感兴趣的话题，或者闲聊。

About

We are a team of Web and mobile technologists working at a small company in Shanghai; we like node.js, Python, Linux, Drupal, Ruby, GIS, Android and many, many other (Open Source) technologies.

关于

我们是位于上海的一个小公司，主营网络和移动技术。我们热爱诸如 Node.js、Python、Linux、Drupal 等各种开源技术。
